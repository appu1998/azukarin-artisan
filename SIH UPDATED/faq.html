Frequently Asked Questions

What is this site all about?

Azukarin works with Indian artisans and craftspersons to contemporize and showcase their beautiful work to the world.

Why do we use it?

When you buy from Azukarin, you can be assured that you're buying the item directly from its creator.

If I have any other query, whom should I contact?

In case of any other query, you may contact the nearest office of the Human Welfare Association Varanasi‟ Service Centre or write to the Human Welfare Association Varanasi, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.‟ Service Centres.

How will the customer verify the genuineness of my product??

If you are a registered user of the Indian Handicraft and Handloom Association, your name would be listed in a list of registered users hosted on the websites of the Human Welfare Association Varanasi. You may ask the customer to verify the genuineness of your label from the said list.
